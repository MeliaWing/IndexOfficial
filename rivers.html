Rivers We Run

One river that we run is the Snake River located along the Washington and Oregon border. The Snake River has many areas where you can just relax and float on the river and many other areas where there are some great rapids (like the Tenmile Rapid area).

The last river that we run is the Wenatchee River. This river, like so may others, has great rapids as well as calm areas of the river. The Wenatchee River stretches between Leavenworth and Wenatchee, Washington.
